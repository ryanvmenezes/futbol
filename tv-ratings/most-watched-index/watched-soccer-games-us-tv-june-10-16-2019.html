Most-watched soccer games on US TV for June 10-16, 2019

Most-watched soccer games on US TV for June 10-16, 2019

Collin Werner June 21, 2019 TV Ratings 20 Comments

The United States Women’s National team dominated the airwaves last week with both of their opening matches coming in as the two most-watched soccer matches of the week. The two matches averaged 3.975 million viewers on the FOX broadcast network and an additional 675,000 viewers on Telemundo for a combined 4.65 million average viewers. FOX is averaging 1.03 million viewers through their first 24 broadcasts of the 2019 FIFA Women’s World Cup with matches on the FOX broadcast network averaging more than 1.7 million viewers and matches on FOX Sports 1 averaging nearly 460,000 viewers.

Univision kicked off their coverage of the 2019 CONCACAF Gold Cup last weekend averaging a strong 982,250 viewers for their first four broadcasts. Naturally, viewership hit its peak for the weekend for Mexico’s 7-0 victory over Cuba, which saw 2 million people tune into Univision for the match.

The FIFA Under 20 World Cup Final between Ukraine and South Korea drew the tournament’s largest audience averaging 423,000 viewers sandwiched between two Women’s World Cup matches.
